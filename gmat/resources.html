GMAT Books

This page lists resources that we feel will help you with your GMAT prep.

Essential

The Official GMAT webpage - Get your facts straight from the official source. Answers all your FAQs. Download the pool of issue and argument topics as well as the GMATPrep software.


The Official Guide for GMAT Review, 12th Edition - You must do all the questions provided in this book although some students find that the questions on the CAT seem harder.

Recommended


Kaplan GMAT Math Workbook - Useful if you feel you need tips on how to attack the questions. Particularly useful if you havn't done math for a while.


Grammar Focus: 50 essential grammar rules - Essential knowledge for multiple-choice sentence correction questions that occur on the GMAT. Supplements the GMAT sentence correction questions on majortests.com

Grammar Smart, 2nd Edition (Smart Guides)

Master the GMAT (CD) 2010 - contains useful tips and tests.

Cracking the GMAT with DVD, 2010 Edition - useful tips and tests although some students find the tests rather easy.
