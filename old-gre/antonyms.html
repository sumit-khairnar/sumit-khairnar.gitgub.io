GRE Antonyms

Antonym questions account for about one quarter of the marks on the verbal section of GRE. Antonyms test your vocabulary.

Each antonym question should take approximately 30 seconds. Our mini tests have 20 questions to be completed in 10 minutes.

Review each test carefully. You will make more progress if you take the trouble to write down all unfamiliar words and look them up.

Directions

Choose the answer key corresponding to the word with a meaning most nearly opposite to the meaning of word in capital letters.

Example:

OBDURATE

(A) amenable
(B) stubborn
(C) rash
(D) vacuous
(E) advanced
