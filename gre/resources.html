GRE Books

This page lists resources that we feel will help you with your GRE prep.

Essential

The Official GRE webpage - Get your facts straight from the official source. Answers all your FAQs. Download the pool of issue and argument topics.

PowerPrep II software - Available free from the ETS. Do these tests early in your preparation to get the look and feel of the examination.


The Official Guide to the GRE revised General Test - You must do all the questions provided in this book although some students find that the questions on the CAT seem harder.


Word Focus: Improve your vocabulary by 1,000 words - Our systematic approach to advanced vocabulary building is particularly helpful for students preparing for the GRE test.

Recommended


Kaplan GRE Math Workbook - Useful if you feel you need tips on how to attack the questions. Particularly useful if you havn't done math for a while.


Kaplan GRE Verbal Workbook - Get some additional practice and useful hints on verbal techniques.


Manhattan GRE Set of 8 Strategy Guides, 2nd Edition - Detailed information on all sections of the test. You can buy the whole set or just the ones you need.
